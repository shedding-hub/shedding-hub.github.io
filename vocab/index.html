About this Vocabulary

The Shedding Hub Vocabulary is a SKOS-based controlled vocabulary designed for standardizing terminology used in studies involving pathogen and biomarker shedding. It provides a structured framework for describing analytes, specimen types, measurement units, reference events, and participant attributes.
